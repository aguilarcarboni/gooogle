Company Policies

1. Work Hours

Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible hours may be arranged with management approval.

2. Dress Code

Business casual attire is expected. Jeans are permitted on Fridays.

3. Time Off

Employees receive 15 days of paid time off per year, accrued monthly. Unused days may be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

4. Remote Work

Remote work is available for eligible positions. Employees must maintain regular communication and meet all job responsibilities when working remotely.
